HOW TO PLAY

Players can roll dice until the number of dice is 1.When dice number equal to 1,that player's score reset to 0.If players click on "HOLD" button before they saw number "1" on dice, their current score transfer to total score.If any player's score is equal to the cap value entered, the game is over.Always "Player 1" starts firstly.
